Cherry Dessert
Serves:  15

## Ingredients
1½ c. flour
½ c. butter
2 T. sugar
¼ t. salt
              ~ mix and press into 9x13 pan – Bake 15 min. @ 350° - Don’t let brown
              ~ cool
Cream:  8 oz. cream cheese
              1 c. pd. Sugar
              1 t. vanilla

## Instructions
Fold in 1 pt. whipped whipping cream (do not sweeten). 
Spread on crust – top w/ 1 can cherry pie filling.
