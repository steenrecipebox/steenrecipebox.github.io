Caramel Popcorn
Serves:  6 quarts of popcorn

## Ingredients
Melt:                2 sticks butter
              2 c. br. Sugar
              ½ c. corn syrup
              1 t. salt
                            ~ bring to boil
              ½ t. soda
              1 t. vanilla

## Instructions
Pour over popcorn
Turn into 2
250° - 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes.
