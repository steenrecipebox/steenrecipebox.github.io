Cheesy Potatoes

Serves:  8

## Ingredients
2 #’s cubed hash browns. 
1 can cr. of chicken soup
2 c. valveeta (10 oz)
1 pt. milk
¼ c. butter
2 T. minced onion
1 t. salt
½ t. pepper
 
## Instructions
Combine all in 9x 13 pan. 
Bake 1 hr. @ 350°
