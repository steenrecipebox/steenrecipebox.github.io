Corn Meal Muffins

## Ingredients
½ c. Corn Meal
1 c. flour
1½ t. baking powder
¼ t. b. soda
¼ c. sugar
½ t. salt
1 c. buttermilk
1 egg
¼ c. vegetable oil
 
## Instructions
Mix all just until blended – let sit 5 minutes
Bake 400°  15 minutes
