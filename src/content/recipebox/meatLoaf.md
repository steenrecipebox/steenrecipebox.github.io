Meat Loaf

## Ingredients
1½ lbs. ground beef
½ lb. ground pork
¼ c. finely chopped onion
2 T. finealy chopped celery
2 t. salt
½ t. poultry seasoning
¼ t. pepper
¼ t. dry mustard
4 slices soft bread
½ c. milk
2 eggs
1 T. worstershire sauce
 
## Instructions
Bake 350°  1 hour
