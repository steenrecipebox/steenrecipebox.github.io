Pork Chops

## Ingredients
3 T. soy sauce
1 egg
1 T. water
½ t. ginger (opt.)
¼ t. garlic powder
              - mix together
 
## Instructions
Spray pan w/ oil. 
Dip chops in above mixture and then in dry bread crumbs. 
Bake 350°  30 min – turn bake 20-30 min. more.
