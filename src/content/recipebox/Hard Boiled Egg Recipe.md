### Hard Boiled Eggs

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- Water to cover

## Instructions

1.  Start with cold water in a pan. 
2.  Bring it to a boil. 
3.  Genly add eggs. (May need to use a slotted spoon)
4.  Bring to a boil again. 
5.  Boil 9 minutes. 
6.  Immerse in cold water. 
7.  Peel and enjoy!
