## Ingredients
- 2 oz. yeast
- 4¼ - 4½ c. sifted flour
- 1½ c. lukewarm water
- 3 T. sugar
- 1 T. salt

## Instructions
1. In large mixer bowl combine yeast, water, sugar and salt. 
2. Add 1¾ c. of flour. 
3. Beat at low speed ½ min. scraping sides of bowl constantly. 
4. Beat 3 min. at high speed. 
5. By hand stir in enough of remaining flour to make a moderately stiff dough. 
6. Kneed ‘til smooth. 
7. Cover and let rest 15 minutes
8. Cut into 16 portions and shape into smooth balls and punch a hole in center of each with floured finger and pull to enlarge hole. 
9. Cover and let rise 20 min.
10. In large kettle combine 1 gallon water and 1 T. salt.
11. Bring to boiling.  Reduce to simmering – cook bagels 4 or 5 at a time for 7 minutes turning once. 
12. Drain on paper towels and place on greased baking sheet. 
13. Bake at 375° for 30-35 minutes.
