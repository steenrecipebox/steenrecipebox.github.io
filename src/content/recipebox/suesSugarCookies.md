Sue’s Sugar Cookies

## Ingredients
1 c. shortening (part butter)
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
¾ c. buttermilk
1 t. soda
2 t. b. powder
½ t. salt
scant t. nutmeg
flour to texture (4 cups)

## Instructions
Bake @ 350°
