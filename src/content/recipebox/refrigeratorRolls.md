Refrigerator Rolls
Serves:  16 rolls
 
## Ingredients
6 – 6½ c. flour
½ c. sugar
2 t. salt
2 T. yeast
½ c. butter
2 c. hot tap water
1 egg
 
## Instructions
Kneed – put in greased bowl over night (Brush top w/ oil while in fridge)
Punch down occasionally – 2 hours before baking shape and let rise (covered)
 
Bake 425° 15-20 minutes
