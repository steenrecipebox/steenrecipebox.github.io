Salmon

## Ingredients
3 T. butter (melted)
1/3 c. plain dried bread crumbs
¼ cup parsley (fresh)
3 T drained capers
1 t. tarragon
2 t. grated lemon peel
¼ t. salt ¼ t. pepper
 
## Instructions
Line jelly roll pan w/ foil – skin side down – place salmon on foil
Mix and pat on top
Roast 30 minutes or until opaque
Serve w/ lemon wedges and parsley
