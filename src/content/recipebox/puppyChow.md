Puppy Chow

## Ingredients
Melt:         1 stick butter
              1 c. choc. Chips
              ½ c. peanut butter
Add:                7-8 c. rice chex

## Instructions
Put all and 2 c. pd. Sugar in large bag – shake well. 
Store in refrigerator.
